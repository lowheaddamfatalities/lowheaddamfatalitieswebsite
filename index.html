Want to learn more about low head dams?

The Low Head Dam Fatalities Database is a project from Brigham Young University to document the dangers of lowhead dams. Awknowledgments: Dr. Rollin Hotchkiss as head of the project, John Guymon for originating the idea of the website, Ed Kern for creating the original version of this website, Dr. Bruce Tschantz for his data on incidents at the dams, Charlie Walbridge for the data from the American Whitewater Association, Dr. Riley Hales and Dr. Dan Ames for their help in the development of this website. Please email lowheaddamfatalities@gmail.com if there are any false or inappropriate entries. Website developed by Paige Gordichuk, and undergraduate student aat Brigham Young University.
